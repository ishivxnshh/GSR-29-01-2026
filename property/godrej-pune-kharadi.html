Godrej Properties has recently acquired a prime 14-acre land parcel in the Kharadi-Wagholi corridor of Pune to develop a premium housing project with a total developable potential of approximately 3.7 million square feet and an estimated revenue potential of ₹4,200 crore.

This project marks Godrej's strategic entry into one of Pune's most sought-after residential micro-markets and is poised to offer high-quality premium homes designed for today's modern lifestyle. The development will primarily consist of premium group housing, ideally positioned to cater to working professionals, families, and investors seeking strong future growth and value.
