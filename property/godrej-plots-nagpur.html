Godrej Plots Nagpur
Godrej Properties is coming up with a new and large-scale plotted development in Nagpur, marking its third and biggest land parcel in the city after the successful Godrej Forest Estate and Godrej Orchard Estate. This upcoming project is spread across approximately 75 acres in a single phase, making it one of the most significant plotted developments in the Nagpur region.

Strategically located near the Samruddhi Mahamarg Circle at Sumthana, the project is poised to benefit from rapid infrastructure growth and long-term capital appreciation. Designed to support premium residential and mixed-use development, the project will offer well-planned plots across multiple size options, catering to individual home builders, institutions, and long-term investors.

Land Parcel & Plot Details

  • Total Land Acquisition: ~75 Acres (Single Phase)
  • Plot Size Range: Approx. 2,200 sq. ft. to 64,300 sq. ft.
  • Development Type: Plotted Development (Residential & Permissible Institutional Use)
  • Project Status: Upcoming Launch

Catchment & Market Strength
